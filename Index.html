I am Tim Berners Lee the founder of www: world wide web

Hi

Sir Timothy John Berners-Lee OM KBE FRS FREng FRSA FBCS, also known as TimBL, is an English computer scientist best known as the inventor of the World Wide Web. He is a Professorial Fellow of Computer Science at the University of Oxford and a professor at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. In 2001 he became a fellow of the Royal society and in 2004 he was Knighted.

In 1994 berners-lee founded the world wide web Consortium (w3c) at the laboratory of computer science (LCS) at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in Boston.
